## DEFINITION

SAN is a deliberately constructed, trust-weighted human graph
designed to compound intelligence and equity across Axiolev LLC
and all downstream OS's.

It is NOT:
  - A casual social graph
  - A public network
  - A voting democracy
  - A loose advisory board
  - A random mastermind group

It IS:
  Curated. Weighted. Strategic. Aligned to cathedral objectives.
  Every member has skin in the game.
  Every member has equity exposure or reputational stake.
  Aligned incentive IS the mechanism. Not a bug.
